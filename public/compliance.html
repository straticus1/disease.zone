Global Compliance Standards

Our distributed ledger infrastructure exceeds international healthcare data protection standards, ensuring your medical data is secure, compliant, and always under your control.

HIPAA Compliance

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act compliance ensures that all Protected Health Information (PHI) is secured according to U.S. federal standards.

Administrative Safeguards

  • Assigned Security Officer with HIPAA expertise
  • Workforce training and access management
  • Information access management protocols
  • Security awareness and training programs
  • Security incident procedures and monitoring
  • Contingency plan and data backup procedures

Physical Safeguards

  • Facility access controls and monitoring
  • Workstation use restrictions and policies
  • Device and media controls for PHI storage

Technical Safeguards

  • Unique user identification and automatic logoff
  • Encryption and decryption of PHI
  • Audit controls and integrity monitoring
  • Person or entity authentication
  • Transmission security and end-to-end encryption

GDPR Ready

General Data Protection Regulation compliance ensures data protection and privacy rights for individuals within the European Union and European Economic Area.

Core Principles

  • Lawful, fair, and transparent processing
  • Purpose limitation and data minimization
  • Accuracy and storage limitation
  • Integrity, confidentiality, and accountability

Individual Rights

  • Right to be informed about data processing
  • Right of access to personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Technical Measures

  • Privacy by design and by default
  • Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)
  • Appointment of Data Protection Officer
  • Breach notification within 72 hours

Security Architecture

Encryption Standards

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • End-to-end encryption for all PHI
  • Hardware Security Modules (HSM) for key management
  • Zero-knowledge architecture implementation

Access Controls

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Attribute-based access control (ABAC)
  • Single Sign-On (SSO) integration
  • Session management and timeout controls

Monitoring & Auditing

  • Real-time security monitoring (SIEM)
  • Automated threat detection and response
  • Comprehensive audit logging
  • Blockchain-based immutable audit trails
  • Regular penetration testing
  • Vulnerability assessments
  • Security incident response procedures
  • 24/7 security operations center (SOC)

## ðŸ“š **CORE TRAINING MODULES**

### **Module 1: HIPAA Fundamentals (Required for All)**
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Frequency:** Annual  
**Format:** Online self-paced with assessment

#### **Learning Objectives:**
- Define HIPAA and its key components
- Identify what constitutes PHI and ePHI
- Understand patient rights under HIPAA
- Recognize covered entities and business associates
- Explain the Security Rule and Privacy Rule

#### **Content Areas:**
1. **HIPAA History and Purpose**
   - Legislative background and intent
   - Healthcare industry challenges addressed
   - Key dates and implementation timeline

2. **Protected Health Information (PHI)**
   - Definition and examples of PHI
   - Electronic PHI (ePHI) specifics
   - De-identification standards
   - Minimum necessary principle

3. **Patient Rights**
   - Right to access medical records
   - Right to request amendments
   - Right to accounting of disclosures
   - Right to restrict uses and disclosures

4. **Covered Entities and Business Associates**
   - Disease Zone's role as business associate
   - Responsibilities and liability
   - Required safeguards and agreements

#### **Assessment:** 15-question quiz (80% passing score)

### **Module 2: Security Safeguards (Required for All)**
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Frequency:** Annual  
**Format:** Online with practical exercises

#### **Learning Objectives:**
- Implement proper access controls
- Use strong authentication methods
- Maintain audit trail awareness
- Follow workstation security procedures
- Understand encryption requirements

#### **Content Areas:**
1. **Administrative Safeguards**
   - Security Officer role and responsibilities
   - Access management procedures
   - Workforce training requirements
   - Incident response procedures

2. **Physical Safeguards**
   - Workstation use restrictions
   - Device and media controls
   - Facility access controls
   - Remote work security

3. **Technical Safeguards**
   - Access control systems
   - Audit controls and logging
   - Integrity controls
   - Transmission security

#### **Practical Exercises:**
- Password creation and management
- Multi-factor authentication setup
- Secure email practices
- Incident reporting simulation

#### **Assessment:** Scenario-based questions (85% passing score)

### **Module 3: Privacy and Confidentiality (Required for All)**
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**Frequency:** Annual  
**Format:** Online with case studies

#### **Learning Objectives:**
- Maintain patient confidentiality
- Handle PHI requests appropriately
- Understand permissible uses and disclosures
- Implement privacy protections

#### **Content Areas:**
1. **Privacy Principles**
   - Confidentiality fundamentals
   - Need-to-know basis
   - Professional discretion
   - Minimum necessary standard

2. **Permissible Uses and Disclosures**
   - Treatment, payment, operations
   - Required disclosures
   - Patient authorization requirements
   - Emergency situations

3. **Privacy Violations**
   - Common violation examples
   - Consequences and penalties
   - Prevention strategies
   - Reporting procedures

#### **Case Studies:**
- Appropriate PHI sharing scenarios
- Privacy violation examples
- Emergency disclosure situations
- Patient request handling

#### **Assessment:** Case study analysis (80% passing score)

### **Module 4: Incident Response and Breach Management (Required for All)**
**Duration:** 1 hour  
**Frequency:** Annual  
**Format:** Interactive online training

#### **Learning Objectives:**
- Recognize potential security incidents
- Follow proper reporting procedures
- Understand breach notification requirements
- Implement containment measures

#### **Content Areas:**
1. **Incident Recognition**
   - Types of security incidents
   - Indicators of potential breaches
   - Suspicious activity identification
   - System anomalies and alerts

2. **Reporting Procedures**
   - Who to contact (Security Officer)
   - When to report (immediately)
   - What information to provide
   - Documentation requirements

3. **Breach Response**
   - Immediate containment steps
   - Investigation procedures
   - Notification timelines
   - Remediation activities

#### **Simulation Exercise:**
- Incident response tabletop exercise
- Breach notification role-play
- Decision-making scenarios

#### **Assessment:** Incident response simulation (90% passing score)
